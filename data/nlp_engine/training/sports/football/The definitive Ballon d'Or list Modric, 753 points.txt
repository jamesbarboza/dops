The definitive Ballon d'Or list: Modric, 753 points...

Luka Modric wins the Ballon d'Or ahead of Cristiano Ronaldo and Antoine Griezmann

Luka Modric was named Ballon d'Or winner and picked up the trophy in Paris after coming comfortably ahead in the voting.

He is the first Croat to win the award and received the trophy with previous holder, Cristiano Ronaldo, an onlooker.

The current Real Madrid side had eight nominees for the award (Modric, Varane, Courtois, Bale, Benzema, Ramos, Marcelo and Isco) from a list of 30. Cristiano came second and Antoine Griezmann was in bronze position.

Barcelona had three representatives (Messi, Suarez and Rakitic) the same as Atletico (Griezmann, Oblak and Godin).
