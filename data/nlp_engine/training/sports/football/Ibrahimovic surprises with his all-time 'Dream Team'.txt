Ibrahimovic surprises with his all-time 'Dream Team'

The full promotion swing is underway for Zlatan Ibrahimovic with his new book I am football.

On one of the pages within, the he commits to paper his all-time Dream Team.

It is an illustrious XI made up of the best teammates he has had in his career.

Barcelona were well represented within his team as Lionel Messi, Ronaldinho and Xavi Hernandez all featured as well as other former Blaugrana players such as Lilian Thuram and Maxwell.

The Brazilian defender is one of the real surprises of the team, although he is a longtime close friend of the Swede.

In addition, Ibra also included a luxury bench which included the likes of Julio Cesar, Andres Iniesta, Fabio Cannavaro, Gennaro Gattuso and Clarence Seedorf.
