The 2018 Ballon d'Or was won by Luka Modric on Monday night and the midfielder brought the prize along to Valdebebas for Tuesday's training session.

He posed for a photo with the award and with his teammates before the day's workout.

His teammates, the coaches and the others who work at Real Madrid's sports centre congratulated him for his success.

Sergio Ramos took his own photo with it and shared it on Instagram.
