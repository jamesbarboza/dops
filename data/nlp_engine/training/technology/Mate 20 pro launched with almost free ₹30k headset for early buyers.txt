Mate 20 pro launched with almost free ₹30k headset for early buyers

Huawei has launched technology-rich Mate 20 pro with an exclusive bundled offer in India. Powered by 7nm Kirin 980 chipset and featuring triple rear camera setup,  it is touted in media reports as the 'King of Smartphones'. Primarily priced at ₹69,990, it is available with Sennheiser PXC 550 headphones for early bird customers at a special price of ₹71,990.
