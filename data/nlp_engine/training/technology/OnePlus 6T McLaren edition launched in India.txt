OnePlus 6T McLaren edition launched in India

OnePlus, in partnership with McLaren, has launched the new OnePlus 6T McLaren edition in India. Featuring a 10GB RAM and super fast 30W Warp Charge technology, the smartphone is priced at ₹50,999. As part of the launch offer, Axis Bank card users can either avail a cashback worth ₹1,500 on all transactions or a cashback worth ₹2,000 on EMI transactions.
