Tech linking humans to machines to be ready in 10 yrs: Musk

Elon Musk in a recent interview said that his AI startup Neuralink is working on a technology to link human brains to machines which will be ready "probably" within 10 years. Musk also said that it will be done "at a micro level" using "a chip and a bunch of tiny wires". "It would make everyone hyper smart," he added.
