OnePlus 6T makers celebrate launch, set a Guinness World Record

After the launch of OnePlus 6T, the company celebrated with a OnePlus 6T mega unboxing event, setting a new Guinness World record for 'Most people unboxing simultaneously'. The event took place on November 1 at Richardson and Cruddas Mumbai, where a total of 559 OnePlus community members came together to unbox the new flagship, as confirmed by Guinness World Records.
