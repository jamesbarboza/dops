Facebook CEO, COO relied on me: Exec on PR firm controversy

Facebook's policy head Elliot Schrage has admitted that he is responsible for hiring a controversial PR firm Definers that targeted the company's critics. "Mark (CEO) and Sheryl (COO) relied on me to manage this without controversy," Schrage said. Adding that he should have known of the decision to expand their mandate, he also said, "I regret my own failure here."
