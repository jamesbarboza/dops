All work, no pray, make Jack a dull boy: SRK on Twitter CEO 

Sharing an image with Twitter CEO Jack Dorsey, actor Shah Rukh Khan has said, "Today @jack made me realise...that all Work and no Pray, would make Jack a dull boy." "Thx for dropping in," he added. Dorsey, who visited the actor's house 'Mannat' in Mumbai on Wednesday, had shared a picture with the house and tweeted, "knock knock."
