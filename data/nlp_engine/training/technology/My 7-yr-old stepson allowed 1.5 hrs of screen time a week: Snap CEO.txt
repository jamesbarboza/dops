My 7-yr-old stepson allowed 1.5 hrs of screen time a week: Snap CEO

The 28-year-old billionaire Co-founder and CEO of Snapchat's parent company Snap, Evan Spiegel said he allows his 7-year-old stepson Flynn 1.5 hours of screen time (time spent on tablets, smartphones) a week. "My parents enforced a no-TV policy until I was almost a teenager," Spiegel added. He married supermodel Miranda Kerr in 2017 after her divorce from actor Orlando Bloom.
