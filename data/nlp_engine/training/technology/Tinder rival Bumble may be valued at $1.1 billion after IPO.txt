Tinder rival Bumble may be valued at $1.1 billion after IPO

Dating app Tinder's rival Bumble could be valued at as low as $1.1 billion, according to Synovus Trust senior portfolio manager Dan Morgan. Earlier in January, it was reported that the Bumble app was seeking $1.5 billion figure in valuation. Reports claimed that Bumble's majority holder Andrey Andreev is talking to banks including JPMorgan about a possible Nasdaq listing.
