Tesla, Ford EVs in China send real-time data to govt: Report

Over 200 carmakers selling electric vehicles in China including Tesla, Volkswagen, Daimler and Ford, send real-time location and other data to the government without car owners' knowledge, according to a report. They send at least 61 data points to government-backed monitoring platforms, it added. The firms say they're complying with local laws which apply only to alternative energy vehicles.
