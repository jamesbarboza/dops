Microsoft's Cortana chief Javier Soltero to quit by year end

Microsoft's digital assistant Cortana division's Vice President, Javier Soltero, will leave the company by the end of 2018. "The past 4 years have been an incredible experience...humbled to have been a part of building something that 100M+ users depend on every day," he said. Soltero joined Microsoft in 2014 after it acquired email startup Acompli for $200 million.
