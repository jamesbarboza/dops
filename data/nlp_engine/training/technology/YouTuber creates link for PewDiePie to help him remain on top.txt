YouTuber creates link for PewDiePie to help him remain on top

YouTuber Logan Paul has created a trackable subscription link for viewers to automatically subscribe to the most-subscribed YouTube channel PewDiePie amidst recent competition from T-Series. Logan, with 1.8 crore subscribers himself, will also contribute 1 Swedish krona to a charity for every click on the link. PewDiePie and T-Series currently have over 7.4 crore subscribers.
