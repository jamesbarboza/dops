Microsoft acquires two video game studios Obsidian, inXile

Microsoft has announced the acquisition of two new video game studios Obsidian Entertainment and inXile Entertainment. "They will continue to operate autonomously with their unique talents, IP and expertise," Microsoft Studios' Corporate VP Matt Booty said. Obsidian is known for 'Fallout: New Vegas' and 'Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic' while inXile is famous for 'Wasteland 2'.
