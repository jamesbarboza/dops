OnePlus 6T pop-up events to be held in 9 cities in India on Nov 2

OnePlus has announced that it will host pop-up events on November 2 post the launch of its flagship smartphone OnePlus 6T for buyers to experience the device. Pop-up events will be held in Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, Kolkata, Pune, Hyderabad, Ahmedabad and Jaipur. Early attendees will receive OnePlus goodies like phone covers, bumpers, sketchbook, 'Never Settle' T-shirts and tote bags.
