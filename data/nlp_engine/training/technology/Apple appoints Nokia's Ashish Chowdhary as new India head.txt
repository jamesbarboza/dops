Apple appoints Nokia's Ashish Chowdhary as new India head

US technology giant Apple has appointed Finnish company Nokia's Chief Customer Operations Officer (CCOO) Ashish Chowdhary as the new Country Manager for India. Chowdhary will succeed Michel Coulomb, who became India head in December 2017. Chowdhary, who joined Nokia in December 2003, holds a Master's degree in Computer Science from the US' Emory University and an MBA from Wharton School.
