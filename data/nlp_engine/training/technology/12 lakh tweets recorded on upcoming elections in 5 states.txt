12 lakh tweets recorded on upcoming elections in 5 states

Twitter has recorded 12 lakh tweets related to the upcoming Assembly elections in five states, in the past week, the microblogging site has said. Twitter has launched initiatives for the elections, including special emojis, live questions and answers on the platform, and #ElectionOnTwitter events. The states going to polling are Chhattisgarh, Mizoram, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan and Telangana.
