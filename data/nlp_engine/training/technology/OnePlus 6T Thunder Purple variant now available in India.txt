OnePlus 6T Thunder Purple variant now available in India

The new Thunder Purple variant of the OnePlus 6T is now available in India on Amazon India and OnePlus online stores. Priced at ₹41,999, the variant will be available with an 8GB RAM and 128GB internal storage. Amazon is offering customers an instant discount of ₹1,500 on Debit and Credit cards as well as a no cost EMI option.
