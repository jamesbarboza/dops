OnePlus, Amazon celebrate 4 yr partnership with OnePlus 6T offers

Celebrating its 4 year partnership with Amazon, OnePlus has announced various offers on the OnePlus 6T. Customers purchasing the smartphone from Amazon via CITI Bank cards will get a ₹1500 cashback along with 6 Months EMI, with no hidden charges. Further, existing OnePlus as well as Apple users can avail upto ₹3000 discount if they upgrade to OnePlus 6T.
