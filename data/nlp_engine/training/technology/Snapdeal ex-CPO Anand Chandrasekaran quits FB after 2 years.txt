Snapdeal ex-CPO Anand Chandrasekaran quits FB after 2 years

Snapdeal's former Chief Product Officer (CPO) Anand Chandrasekaran has quit Facebook after joining the company two years ago. An active angel investor of the Indian startup ecosystem, Anand Chandrasekaran was the director of platform and product partnerships at Facebook. Chandrasekaran reportedly quit the company to explore opportunities in the US city San Francisco's Bay Area.
