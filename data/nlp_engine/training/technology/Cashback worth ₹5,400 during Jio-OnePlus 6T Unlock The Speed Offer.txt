Cashback worth ₹5,400 during Jio-OnePlus 6T Unlock The Speed Offer


Ahead of OnePlus 6T's launch, Reliance Jio has announced 'Jio-OnePlus 6T Unlock The Speed Offer'. Jio customers who purchase OnePlus 6T will get cashback worth ₹5,400 on the first recharge of ₹299, divided in 36 vouchers worth ₹150 each. Customers can redeem these on subsequent recharges of ₹299, availing the benefits of the plan at an effective price of ₹149.
