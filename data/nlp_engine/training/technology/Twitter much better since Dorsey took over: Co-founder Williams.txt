Twitter much better since Dorsey took over: Co-founder Williams

Talking about Twitter CEO Jack Dorsey, the microblogging site's Co-founder Ev Williams has said, "I think the company's in a much better place than a few years ago when he took over." Adding that there's a lot of external evidence for the same, he also said, "I've been impressed with what he's done." Dorsey took over as CEO in 2015.
