Hackers use Elon Musk's name to rob Twitter users

Hackers used Tesla CEO Elon Musk's name to ask Twitter users to make bitcoin payments by promoting an advert on Twitter. They hacked several verified twitter accounts including those of British fashion retailer Matalan and film distributor Pathe UK to promote the fake advert. They further used other hacked verified accounts to claim they have received bitcoin from Musk.
