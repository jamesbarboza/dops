Twitter posts 29% increase in revenue at $758 million in Q3

Twitter has posted a 29% year-over-year increase in revenue at $758 million in the third quarter of 2018. Advertising revenue also saw a 29% year-over-year increase to $650 million, while total ad engagements increased 50% for the same period. Twitter also revealed its monthly active users reduced to 326 million from 330 million for the same period in 2017.
