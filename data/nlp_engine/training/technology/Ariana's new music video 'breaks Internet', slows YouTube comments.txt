Ariana's new music video 'breaks Internet', slows YouTube comments

American singer-songwriter Ariana Grande released a music video 'thank u, next' on Friday, which delayed the posting of YouTube comments. "The video was so good, it broke the internet," YouTube tweeted, adding "Comments are still working on the video, they're just delayed!" The video reportedly received 1 million views in 35 minutes making it the fastest to reach the mark.
