120 mn FB accounts hacked, messages from 81,000 on sale: Report

Hackers gained access to private messages of nearly 120 million Facebook accounts and published messages from 81,000 accounts for generating money, a BBC report said. "The hackers offered to sell access for 10 cents per account. However, their advert has since been taken offline," the report added. Users from Russia, Ukraine, UK, US, and Brazil were mainly affected.
