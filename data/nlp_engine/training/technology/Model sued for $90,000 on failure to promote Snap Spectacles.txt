Model sued for $90,000 on failure to promote Snap Spectacles

Luka Sabbat, a model and a social media influencer with 1.4 million Instagram followers, is being sued for $90,000 by the firm PR Consulting on failure to promote Snap Spectacles. They claimed Sabbat failed to uphold clauses of their $60,000 contract. He allegedly made one Instagram Post and one Story while he was expected to make four on the platform.
