Man spends ₹3.5 lakh to put Zimbabwe on Google Street View

A 37-year-old Zimbabwean Tawanda Kanhema has spent $5,000 (over ₹3.5 lakh) of his own money to put his country on Google Street View. Kanhema funded the travel and production costs related to the project while the equipment was loaned from Google and camera company Insta360. He covered the footage in two weeks and has uploaded over 500 miles of coverage.
