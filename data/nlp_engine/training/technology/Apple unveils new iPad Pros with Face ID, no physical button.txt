Apple unveils new iPad Pros with Face ID, no physical button

Apple on Tuesday unveiled new iPad Pros that have no physical button, have larger screen options of 11 inch and 12.9 inch, and come with Face ID feature for unlocking. Apple has, for the first time, introduced USB-C port in the iPads. While the smaller 11-inch iPad Pro starts from $799, the larger 12.9-inch iPad Pro starts from $999.
