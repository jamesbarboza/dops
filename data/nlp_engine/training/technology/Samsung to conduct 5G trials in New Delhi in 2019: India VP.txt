Samsung to conduct 5G trials in New Delhi in 2019: India VP

Telecom equipment maker Samsung will conduct large-scale 5G trials in New Delhi in the first quarter of the next year, according to the company's India Senior VP Srini Sundararajan. He also said, "We are working very closely with the Department of Telecom...we will also work with many partners on use cases." The technology holds huge potential for India, he added.
