YouTuber PewDiePie raises ₹1.6 crore in one day for kids in India

Felix Kjellberg, who runs YouTube's most-subscribed channel PewDiePie, has raised £181,301 (₹1.6 crore) in just a day for Indian charity CRY, which works with underprivileged children. The 29-year-old urged fans not to post offensive comments about India amid competition with Indian label T-Series for YouTube's top spot. T-Series is currently trailing PewDiePie by around 400,000 subscribers.
