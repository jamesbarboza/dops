Facebook rolls out 'Diwali Stories' for Indian users

Facebook is rolling out a collective 'Diwali Story' for Indian users on its Stories feature. The Story, titled ‘Diwali’, will be visible on top of the news feed for 24 hours only. Users, who will be able to see a collective Diwali Story from Facebook and their friends, can choose to create separate Diwali Stories and personal Stories.
