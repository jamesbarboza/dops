WhatsApp India head will be named by year end: CEO 

Facebook-owned messaging company WhatsApp's CEO Chris Daniels has said, "We’ll have a head of WhatsApp India named by the end of the year." Talking about tracing fake news on the platform, he further said, “We want to keep WhatsApp the way it was built to be...a place for private conversations.” Daniels is on his second visit to India.
