Twitter thought I got hacked: Elon Musk after Bitcoin tweet

Elon Musk on Tuesday said that Twitter briefly locked his account because the microblogging site suspected his account had been hacked. "Twitter thought I got hacked & locked my account haha," Musk tweeted. It comes after Musk posted a picture featuring an anime drawing of a girl wearing Bitcoin-themed clothing with caption, "Wanna buy some Bitcoin?"
