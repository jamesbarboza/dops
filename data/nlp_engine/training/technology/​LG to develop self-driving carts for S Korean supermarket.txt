LG Electronics has announced that it will co-develop a smart self-driving cart for use in South Korean supermarket chain, Emart. The carts, which will follow customers around the store as they shop, will feature built-in barcode scanner, as well as a display to show users' shopping list. Further, the proof-of-concept testing will begin in the first half of next year.
