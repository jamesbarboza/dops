Samsung accused of using DSLR camera photo to promote phone

Samsung has been accused of using a DSLR photo for promoting the camera quality of Galaxy A8 Star on their Malaysian website. Photographer Dunja Djudjic said, "My first reaction was to burst...into laughter. Just look at the Photoshop job they did on my face and hair!" Samsung also allegedly changed the portrait's background to falsely depict the phone's blurring capabilities.
